Botz Hackathon





About the Hackathon

Welcome to the Botz Hackathon 2024! This hackathon is a platform for creative minds to come together and tackle real-world challenges through innovative solutions. Whether you're a seasoned developer, a designer, or someone with a passion for problem-solving, this event is for you. Get ready to unleash your creativity, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and make a difference!

Objective

The objective of Botz Hackathon 2024 is to develop technological solutions that empower communities and address pressing social issues. Participants will work in teams to create projects that have a tangible impact on society, whether it's enhancing accessibility, promoting sustainability, improving healthcare, or fostering education.

Registration Rules and Timelines

Register your team of 2-4 members on our website [Registration Link]. Registration closes on April 10th, 2024.

Venue and Timing

Venue for the hackathon is Loyola-ICAM College of Engineering and Technology and the Hackathon is on 15th and 16th of April.
